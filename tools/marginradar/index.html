FAQ

What is break-even ROAS?‚ñæ

Break-even ROAS is the minimum return on ad spend where profit after ad cost is zero. Above this level you are profitable, below it you lose money.

What does Max CAC mean?‚ñæ

Max CAC is the highest acquisition cost you can pay per order while still meeting your target margin assumptions.

Should I include VAT in price inputs?‚ñæ

Yes‚Äîuse your real selling economics consistently. If your team tracks margins ex-VAT, keep all inputs aligned to that model.

Can I use MarginRadar for Google and Meta ads?‚ñæ

Yes. The tool is channel-agnostic because it models unit economics, not platform-specific metrics.

How often should I update assumptions?‚ñæ

Update assumptions whenever COGS, shipping, discount mix, or AOV changes. Weekly refresh is a good operating rhythm.

Is any data sent to a server?‚ñæ

No. MarginRadar runs in your browser and does not require API calls or account login.
