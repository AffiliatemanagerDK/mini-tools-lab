FAQ

What does “Reorder by” date represent?▾

It is the latest date you should place a purchase order to avoid projected stockout based on your current velocity and lead time.

How is runway calculated?▾

Runway is current stock divided by average units sold per day. The timeline then maps that runway to stockout and reorder markers.

Should I enter sales per day or week?▾

Use whichever mode is shown in the tool and keep it consistent with your current velocity period.

How does safety stock affect results?▾

Higher safety stock moves your reorder date earlier, reducing stockout risk but increasing inventory buffer needs.

Can StockPulse handle seasonality?▾

Use current season-adjusted velocity for best short-term decisions, then re-check frequently as demand shifts.

Does StockPulse store my numbers?▾

No. Calculations are browser-side only and your inputs stay local.
