    FAQ

    What is checkout friction?▾

    Checkout friction is the effort and uncertainty users experience during checkout, including too many fields, extra steps, required account creation, and missing trust signals.

    How is the score calculated?▾

    The score combines weighted penalties from form fields, steps, account requirements, payment flexibility, speed, and UX safeguards to estimate relative friction.

    What is a good friction score?▾

    Lower is better. A score under 30 is typically smooth, 30-55 is moderate, and above 55 usually indicates strong abandonment risk.

    Can this replace analytics?▾

    No. This is a fast diagnostic layer. Use it with analytics and session recordings for final prioritization.

    What should I fix first?▾

    Start with the largest weighted contributors: reduce field count, remove forced account creation, and simplify payment and shipping clarity.

    Is data sent to servers?▾

    No. All calculations run in your browser with no API calls.
