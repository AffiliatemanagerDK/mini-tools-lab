FAQ

What is PromoGuard best used for?▾

PromoGuard compares full-price and discount scenarios so you can see margin and profit trade-offs before launch.

Can I test both percentage and fixed discounts?▾

Yes. You can switch between percent-off and fixed-amount discounts to compare impact.

Why can revenue rise while profit falls?▾

Discounts can increase conversion but still reduce contribution margin per order. PromoGuard highlights that cannibalization risk.

What inputs matter most?▾

Price, COGS, shipping, payment fees, ad cost, and discount depth are the key drivers of net profitability.

How should I interpret “Warning” or “Critical”?▾

Those states indicate the promo likely damages economics under your assumptions and should be redesigned or limited.

Is PromoGuard suitable for DTC teams?▾

Yes. It is designed for practical DTC pricing checks and runs fully client-side.
